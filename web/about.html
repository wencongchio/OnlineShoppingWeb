About Skin Bliss

Skin Bliss have been introducing skin care products for the past 20 years. We start our company at 3rd of July,1988 and aim to build our career becoming the world No.1 online trading skin care company.

Rachel Chio, the founder of Skin Bliss mention that the main reason for her to build up this career is to help more people to take care of their skin and health even they are staying at home.

" Now, Skin Bliss have become a world known company and wishes to help more people to take care their skin, but for us, I think that our journey has just begun. ", said Rachel Chio.
